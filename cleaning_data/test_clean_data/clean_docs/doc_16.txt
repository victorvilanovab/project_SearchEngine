middle shabele somali shabeellaha dhexe   is an administrative region gobolka in southern somalia as part of the former benadir region its capital was mogadishu until the mid s when the town of jowhar became the capital it is bordered by the somalian regions of galguduud hiiraan lower shabele and banaadir and the indian ocean

it is named after the river shabeelle that passes this region
