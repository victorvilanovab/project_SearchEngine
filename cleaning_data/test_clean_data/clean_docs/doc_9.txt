oingo boingo is the first official release from the band oingo boingo this was also the first release of the song only a lad  in a cost saving move the label chose to release the bands demo recordings rather than paying for a studio session  the song selection includes three original compositions by danny elfman as well as a ska inflected cover of bluesman willie dixons violent love  the song only a lad was subsequently rerecorded and issued as the title track on their first long playing album

the cover includes an illustration of a cat by artist louis wain from the later stages of his schizophrenia
